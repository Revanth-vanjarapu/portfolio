About

I started my journey in software engineering back in November 2021, when I joined Singlepoint Solutions as an Associate Software Engineer. I began by working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, building and improving websites and applications that focused on clean, responsive design and solid user experience.

In 2023, I moved into a larger role at Volta, a subsidiary of Singlepoint, where I’ve been working as a Software Engineer on their ride-hailing platform. I’ve been creating user-friendly interfaces using the Ionic framework, contributing to everything from the admin panel to the customer-facing apps and the website itself. Around this time, I also started diving deeper into full-stack development—especially the MERN stack—to broaden my skills and stay in step with the ever-evolving tech world.

What I really enjoy is the mix of design and development—bringing interfaces to life in a way that’s both visually appealing and technically solid. I’m always curious, always learning, and always looking for ways to build better digital experiences.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me outside, reading something interesting, exploring new tech, or brainstorming ideas for whatever’s next.
